Welcome!

Please read the following information carefully before you decide to take part in this task.

Description

We are conducting a research study to evaluate the quality of an audio processing algorithm. If you agree to participate, you will be asked to fill out a brief set of questions about your hearing ability and the listening setup you intend to use for this study. You will then be asked to evaluate a series of short audio recordings.

This survey contains four easy questions designed to judge if the respondent carefully listens to all audio. Pay is conditional on answering at least three of these easy questions correctly.

Privacy

The entire interaction is completely anonymous. We will NOT collect any personally identifiable information. Your participation in this study does not involve any risk to you beyond that of your everyday life.

Consent

By checking I Agree, you confirm you are willing to participate in this research. However, you are free to withdraw your participation at any time.




Contact

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact the principal investigator, Patrick O'Reilly, at patrick.oreilly2024@northwestern.edu



Preliminary Questions

1. Have you ever been diagnosed with hearing loss or a hearing disorder? You will not be penalized for answering "yes."


2. Which listening device are you currently using?


3. Rate how quiet your current listening environment is on a scale from 1 to 5, with 1 being very noisy and 5 being very quiet.
